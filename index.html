About us

Codex is the coding club initiated by the electronics and communication engineering department of Adi Shankara institute of engineering and technology, Kalady. The club aims to inculcate the importance of coding in the current scenario as well as to gather the students interested in coding. The club shall conduct various coding related activities like workshops, competitions, hackathons etc in order for the students to explore more about coding

Mission

  • To establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding.
  • To regularly conduct events in order to inculcate students with the importance of coding in current scenario
  • To help gather students passionate in coding, share their views among each other and initiate ideas and projects

Vision

Empower the student community with coding skills, enhance their problem solving expertise and explore them to an ocean of opportunities establish a coding culture on campus, reaching every student passionate about coding.

#1 milestone

The club “codex” was inaugurated on 19th December 2022 along with the logo launch. The chief guest for the day was Dr. Satheesh Chandran C, he is currently the lead AI solutions at tata consultancy at Kochi. The inauguration started with the prayer and lamp lighting. The vice chairman of the club, Manikandan A R gave a hearty welcome to the dignitaries with a welcome speech. The mentor of the club, Ms. Arya Paul gave a basic idea about the insight of the club. Principal, Prof. K T Subramanian gave the presidential address, followed by inauguration and speech by the chief guest. HOD of the ECE department spoke about his thoughts on Codex, followed by the chairman, Devadatt who shared the core committee of the club. A class was taken by the chief guest on the scope of ML. The inauguration was concluded with the vote of thanks by Jaison.
